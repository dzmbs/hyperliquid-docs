# Deposited fiat

Fiat on-ramp is powered by Swapped.com, an independent service provider. For any questions or issues with your deposit, contact Swapped's dedicated support team directly, available 24/7.

* Live chat: Click the "Open live chat" button on <https://swapped.com/contact-us>
* Email: <support@swapped.com>

Useful links:

* Supported payment methods: <https://swapped.com/payment-methods>
* Fees: <https://swapped.com/fees>
